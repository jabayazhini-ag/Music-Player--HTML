Maestro Ilaiyaraaja



About

He is nicknamed Isaignani (The Musical Genius) and is often referred to as Maestro, the title conferred to him by the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, London. He is a legendary Indian composer, conductor,and songwriter, known for revolutionizing the Indian music industry. With a career spanning over four decades, he has composed over 7,000 songs and provided scores for more than 1,000 films. His unique blend of classical Indian music with electronic orchestration has left an indelible mark on the world of music, making him one of the most revered and influential composers in the history of Indian cinema. He is a recipient of five National Film Awards. He was bestowed with the Padma Bhushan, by the Government of India in 2010 and and in 2018 he was presented with second-highest civilian award - the Padma Vibhushan.
